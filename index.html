Who Is The Black Panther?

He is a comic book character named T'Challa. In the books, T'Challa is King of the nation of Wakanda, which is one of the most advanced civilizations on Earth, as well as the Black Panther, protector of Wakanda, fighter for justice, and occasionally beats up his house guests.



Seriously, in his first ever appearance, he invited the Fantastic Four to chill, but that wasn’t on T'Challa’s menu . One by one, he beat the team, which was mad rude, but he had a good reason. They made up...cuz comics.



In the upcoming film, T'Challa has donned the mantle of Black Panther, but he has not become king. Yet.
